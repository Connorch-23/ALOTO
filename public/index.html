Our Mission

ALOTO is committed to creating a space where people with unique gifts can cultivate independence and forge enduring friendships. Through a variety of recreational activities, ALOTO ensures that everyone can experience joy and excitement.

Our Story

Our journey began on July 25, 2002, a pivotal moment that would redefine our lives forever—the birth of our only son, Bryce Alexander Hayes. Bryce's arrival filled our hearts with unimaginable joy and excitement, marking him not only our first and only son but the first grandson in the family.

In the months following his birth, routine doctor visits revealed that Bryce wasn’t growing as expected. He had a cherubic face framed by beautiful almond-shaped brown eyes. At five months, after numerous tests, doctors diagnosed Bryce with Down Syndrome. The news was devastating, shattering all the dreams and aspirations we had envisioned for him. I vividly remember the overwhelming sorrow I felt for myself as tears flowed freely. Lying on the examination table, Bryce looked up at me, undoubtedly puzzled by my distress. His gaze and a radiant smile sparked a profound realization within me—I had been engulfed in self-pity, not considering the needs and future of my precious son.

From that moment, Bryce became my teacher. He has grown into the happiest, most handsome, and intelligent young man I know. Bryce taught me to see beyond appearances, to empathize deeply with others, and to cherish and embrace life with all its uncertainties. His innate kindness and compassion exceed anything I could have ever imagined. This profound transformation in our perspective inspired our family to take action and give back.

Bryce is like an angel on Earth, radiating love and kindness wherever he goes. His ability to love unconditionally has taught us to do the same, making him a beloved figure in our community. His presence has turned him into a local celebrity, beloved by all who know him.

Driven by Bryce's impact, we are launching "A League of Their Own" (ALOTO). Our vision for ALOTO is to create a welcoming space where everyone can run, play, and be free. We aim to offer a place where individuals can fully express themselves, enjoy life, and build lifelong friendships through recreational activities that foster their athletic spirits. Join us in celebrating every individual's unique journey and creating a community where everyone belongs and thrives.
